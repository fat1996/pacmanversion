Instructions on how to use this reminder tool:
  • IMPORTANT: Please change your browser settings to allow notifications for this application.
  • You can add up to 3 reminders at a time.
  • ADDING A REMINDER:
    Add your reminder in the textbox provided, and then select a time interval at which you want your notification to appear. Press the Add reminder! button.
  • If no time is chosen, it defaults to 5 mins.
  • HOW TO START RECEIVING THE REMINDER AS A DESKTOP NOTIFICATION:
    Simply press Trigger and your added reminder will continue to appear as a desktop notification based on the time interval selected.
  • IF YOU WANT TO STOP THE NOTIFICATIONS:
    Press the Remove reminder button
